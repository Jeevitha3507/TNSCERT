Chapter 1 — The Wonderful World of Science

Have you ever wondered why the sky is blue, or why the stars twinkle at night? Have you ever watched a flower bloom and marvelled at how it knows when to open?

These are just a few of the many mysteries that science helps us unravel. The most wonderful thing about science is that it is everywhere — from the depths of the ocean to the vastness of outer space.

💡 Did You Know? The word "science" comes from the Latin scientia, meaning knowledge. Science began when humans first asked why!

1.1 What is Science?

Science is a systematic way of understanding the natural world through observation, experimentation, and logical reasoning.
