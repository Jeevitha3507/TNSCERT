Chapter 1 — The Wonderful World of Science

Have you ever wondered why the sky is blue, or why the stars twinkle at night? Have you ever watched a flower bloom and marvelled at how it knows when to open?

wondered
VERB · ENGLISH
To feel curiosity or wonder; to think about something with interest and amazement.
தமிழ்: ஆச்சரியப்படுதல்

These are just a few of the many mysteries that science helps us unravel. The most wonderful thing about science is that it is everywhere — from the depths of the ocean to the vastness of outer space, from what is cooking in the kitchen to what is happening on the playground.

Some of the most groundbreaking discoveries have often come from unexpected places — a falling apple, a bubbling kettle, a glowing sample of radium.

💡 Did You Know? The word "science" comes from the Latin scientia, meaning knowledge. Science began when humans first started asking why!

1.1 What is Science?

Science is a systematic way of understanding the natural world through observation, experimentation, and logical reasoning. It helps us explain phenomena around us.
